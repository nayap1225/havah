How Planets
Work in HAVAH

  • HAVAH GOVERNANCE
    Vote on important matters on decisions
    related to the running of the ecosystem.
  • INTERCHAIN ENABLER
    Acts as the messenger during
    the interchain transmission process.
  • NETWORK NODE
    Acts as nodes on the HAVAH main
    network (to be announced).
  • DESTRIBUTED NFT SOURCE
    REPOSITORY
    Standardizes the metadata and source
    data between different NFTs and stores
    them
    on the repository.

  • PVP STRATEGY GAME CRYPTO GEARS
    Battle it out with other players using various
    interchain NFTs that can be used inside
    PvP strategy games!
  • PROJECT SPQ Are you ready
    for the SPQ?
    Meet HAVAH's first premium NFT, SPQ!
    Minted SPQ can be used for boosting
    your
    contribution rewards or in DApps.
  • TCG GAME Project P2C
    NFT collected on various networks
    are assigned
    new unique attributes when
    transferred to HAVAH.
    Show off your NFT
    to holders from all over the world
    and battle it
    out against others.
